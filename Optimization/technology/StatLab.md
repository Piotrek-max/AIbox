## Statistical Learning 
A laboratory introduces various concepts and techniques in statistical learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning, regression, classification, clustering, and dimensionality reduction. 
The lab provides hands-on experience with real-world datasets and emphasizes the application of statistical learning methods to solve practical problems.
